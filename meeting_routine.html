Tier 2 Cadence

Weekly Planning Meeting

Frequency: Weekly Cycle

Meeting Purpose

Focuses on aligning capacity planning, locking the upcoming week's schedule, and proactively identifying and mitigating potential resource bottlenecks or project schedule conflicts.

Key Discussion Topics:
  • • Capacity Tool review and resource allocation
  • • Schedule baseline updates and locking
  • • Risk management and mitigation planning

Participants & Roles

PM
PMO (Project Management Office) Owns the capacity model, updates the master schedule, and highlights critical risks.
LD
Site Leadership Validates resource availability, approves local plans, and commits to the locked timeline.
Tier 1 Cadence

Weekly Follow-Up Meeting

Frequency: Weekly Cycle

Meeting Purpose

The core operational pulse. Designed to track weekly task execution, address immediate blockers, and ensure team alignment with the locked project schedules.

Key Discussion Topics:
  • • Weekly MS Planner board updates
  • • Immediate roadblock identification and resolution
  • • Task prioritization and next steps alignment

Participants & Roles

LD
Site Leadership Provides operational guidance, unblocks tasks, and manages local team support.
TM
Core Team Members Provide honest progress updates, flag dependencies, and execute daily tasks.
Weekly Sprint Cycle

Sprint Technical Discussion

Frequency: Weekly Cycle

Meeting Purpose

A dedicated, highly collaborative space for resolving deep technical issues, standardizing engineering/analytical practices, and unblocking complex deliverables.

Key Discussion Topics:
  • • Deep-dive into technical issues and blockers
  • • Documentation, lab method, or standard review
  • • Knowledge sharing and peer-to-peer support

Participants & Roles

TA
Technical Leads & Experts Lead the technical troubleshooting, enforce standards, and share best practices.
TM
Core Team Members Collaborate on solutions, review peers' work, and implement standard practices.
Tier 3 Cadence

Weekly Decision Making

Frequency: Weekly Cycle

Meeting Purpose

The executive decision-making engine. Focuses on resolving escalated issues from Tier 1 and Tier 2, reviewing high-impact demands, and approving critical timeline adjustments.

Key Discussion Topics:
  • • Critical delay approvals and postponement reviews
  • • High-impact project prioritization and resource conflicts
  • • Escalated operational blocker resolution

Participants & Roles

LD
Executive Leadership Reviews escalations, holds final decision-making authority, and approves schedule changes.
PM
PMO (Project Management Office) Presents data-driven impact assessments and ensures decisions are documented.
Tier 3 Strategic Loop

Quarterly Indicators & Strategy

Frequency: Quarterly Cycle (Every 3 Months)

Meeting Purpose

A strategic review session held every quarter. It is dedicated to evaluating global indicators, assessing overall program performance, and identifying systemic opportunities for continuous process improvement.

Strategic Focus Areas:
  • • Quarterly KPI performance and trend analysis
  • • Global resource capacity vs. pipeline forecast
  • • Continuous improvement initiative prioritization and strategic planning

Participants & Roles

EL
Executive & Site Leadership Review performance against strategic goals, align resources, and set the next quarter's focus.
GO
Governance & PMO Owners Present consolidated global analytics, identify systemic bottlenecks, and propose improvements.
