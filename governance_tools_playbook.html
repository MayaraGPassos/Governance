M365 Licensing & Access Setup

Essential steps to authorize users and provision governance tools.

  • 1
    License Verification: Verify that the user has a valid Microsoft 365 license (F3, E3, or E5) assigned in the Admin Center.
  • 2
    Access Request Form: Open the "M365 Resource Request" form, enter user details, and select the required applications (Planner, Teams, SharePoint).
  • 3
    Manager Approval: Submit the form. The system routes the request to the direct manager for formal approval before licensing is active.
    • Critical Success Factor

    Ensure the user's M365 email matches their Active Directory profile exactly to prevent automated synchronization failures across Teams and MS Planner.

    MS Planner Project Standardization

    Guidelines for structuring buckets, tasks, and team tracking.

  • 1
    Standard Bucket Creation: Build buckets corresponding to standard project phases (e.g., Initiation, Planning, Execution, Review, Closeout).
  • 2
    Task Assignment Rules: Every task must have a single assigned owner, clear start/end dates, and a defined list of deliverables in the checklist.
  • 3
    Teams Integration: Link the Planner board directly to the dedicated MS Teams channel tab to facilitate quick access and real-time collaboration.
    • Critical Success Factor

    Do not create ad-hoc buckets outside the standard framework. Standard bucket naming is required for Power BI to compile global performance metrics accurately.

    Work Log Application Best Practices

    10 essential steps for logging time, tracking capacity, and managing workload.

  • 1
    Daily Log Discipline: Team members must log their hours daily in the Work Log App to prevent retrospective estimation errors.
  • 2
    Task-to-Project Association: Every logged hour must be linked to a specific, active project ID and task category.
  • 3
    Non-Project Activity Logging: Log administrative tasks, training, and meetings under designated "Indirect Time" categories.
  • 4
    Standard Workday Capacity: Log exactly 8 hours per standard workday (or actual overtime hours if pre-approved by the leader).
  • 5
    Weekly Submission Deadline: Submit all weekly logs by Friday at 5:00 PM local time to allow weekend data processing.
  • 6
    Manager Review & Approval: Leaders must review and approve team logs by Monday at 12:00 PM to lock the week's data.
  • 7
    Log Rejection Workflow: If a log is rejected, the leader must provide comments and the user must resubmit within 24 hours.
  • 8
    Real-Time Capacity Check: Use the App's dashboard to monitor your personal capacity vs. allocated project hours.
  • 9
    Historical Data Auditing: The PMO will audit historical logs monthly to refine future project capacity planning models.
  • 10
    Absence and Leave Tracking: Log planned vacation, sick leave, or national holidays in advance to maintain accurate capacity forecasts.

    • Power BI Analytics & Reporting

    10 key steps to analyze data, track performance, and leverage dashboards.

  • 1
    Automated Data Refresh: Power BI dashboards refresh automatically twice a day (at 8:00 AM and 6:00 PM local time).
  • 2
    Access and Workspaces: Access reports via the official "Governance Workspace" on the Power BI cloud service.
  • 3
    Project Progress View: Use the "Timeline Tracking" tab to compare baseline schedules against actual task completion.
  • 4
    Capacity and Overload Alert: Review the "Resource Workload" visual to identify overallocated teams and potential bottlenecks.
  • 5
    Filter and Drill-Down: Apply filters by Site, Department, Project Manager, or specific dates to isolate localized performance.
  • 6
    Weekly Metric Review: Leaders must consult the "Tier 1/Tier 2 Operational Dashboard" prior to the weekly meetings.
  • 7
    Exporting Reports: Export visual data to Excel or PowerPoint for local presentations or stakeholder alignment.
  • 8
    Data Discrepancy Flagging: Report any visual data errors to the PMO immediately via the "Data Issue" button.
  • 9
    Custom Alert Subscription: Set up personal email alerts for critical schedule changes or capacity overloads.
  • 10
    KPI Baseline Audits: Use the "Historical KPI" tab to track long-term trends and validate the 79% lead time reduction.
